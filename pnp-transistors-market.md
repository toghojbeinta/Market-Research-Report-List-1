<p><p>The PNP transistor market is primarily categorized into Bipolar Transistors and Field-Effect Transistors (FETs). Bipolar Transistors, which include PNP types, operate through the movement of charge carriers (holes and electrons) and are essential in analog signal amplification and switching applications. Field-Effect Transistors, on the other hand, control current through an electric field and are favored for their high input impedance and efficiency in digital circuits. Both types are pivotal in electronics, influencing various industries from consumer electronics to automotive systems.</p></p>
