<p><ul><li>Positive Displacement Flow Meters</li><li>Turbine Flow Meters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Liquid flow meters are essential for measuring the flow rate of liquids in various industries. Positive displacement flow meters operate by trapping a specific volume of liquid and counting the number of times it fills a chamber, offering high accuracy for low flow conditions. Turbine flow meters utilize a rotating impeller, where the flow rate is proportional to the speed of rotation, making them suitable for higher flow rates and offering rapid response times. Both types cater to specific application needs.</p></p>
