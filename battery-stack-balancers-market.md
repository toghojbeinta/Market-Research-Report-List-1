<p><strong>The Battery Stack Balancers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Balancing Systems</li><li>Passive Balanced Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Battery stack balancers are essential in managing battery performance and longevity. Active balancing systems redistribute energy between cells, optimizing their charge levels for enhanced efficiency and lifespan. They allow for controlled energy transfer, minimizing waste. On the other hand, passive balancing systems dissipate excess charge from higher voltage cells as heat, ensuring cell uniformity but at the cost of energy loss. Both types serve to maintain battery pack integrity, crucial for applications in electric vehicles and renewable energy storage.</p></p>
