<p><strong>The Aluminium Die Casting Machinery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Chamber Machine</li><li>Cold Chamber Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aluminium Die Casting Machinery market is primarily divided into two types: hot chamber and cold chamber machines. Hot chamber machines are suited for alloys with lower melting points, employing a continuous injection process to enhance productivity and efficiency. In contrast, cold chamber machines are designed for materials with higher melting points, featuring a separate melting furnace that minimizes contamination and allows for versatile casting applications. Both types cater to different manufacturing needs and are essential in producing high-quality aluminium parts.</p></p>
