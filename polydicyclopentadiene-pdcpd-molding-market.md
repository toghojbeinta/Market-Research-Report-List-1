<p><p>The Polydicyclopentadiene (PDCPD) molding market features several key types of processes. Reactive Injection Molding (RIM) involves the mixing of resin and hardener before injection into molds. Resin Transfer Molding (RTM) uses a pre-formed dry fiber mold infused with resin. Vacuum Assisted Forming (VARI) enhances resin flow using vacuum pressure. Variable Vacuum Permeation Molding (FI) employs varying vacuum levels for better impregnation. Compression Molding (SMC) presses resin sheets into molds, while Wet Molding (WCM) involves applying resins to wet fibers. Prepreg Moulding (PCM) uses pre-impregnated materials. Other processes encompass various innovative techniques.</p></p>
