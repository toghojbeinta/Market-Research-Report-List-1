<p><p>The esophageal dilator market is primarily categorized into mechanical esophageal dilators and balloon esophageal dilators. Mechanical esophageal dilators are designed with rigid structures that gradually widen the esophagus, addressing strictures and other obstructions effectively. In contrast, balloon esophageal dilators utilize inflating balloons to expand the esophagus gently, allowing for a more controlled and graduated dilation process. Both types aim to improve patient outcomes by enhancing esophageal function and relieving dysphagia, catering to varying clinical needs and preferences.</p></p>
