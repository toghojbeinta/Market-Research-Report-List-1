<p><strong>The Flexible Manufacturing System Solutions Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sequential FMS</li><li>Random FMS</li><li>Dedicated FMS</li><li>Engineered FMS</li><li>Modular FMS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flexible Manufacturing System (FMS) solutions can be categorized into several types based on their operational characteristics. Sequential FMS operates in a fixed sequence, streamlining tasks but limiting adaptability. Random FMS allows for variable production paths, enhancing flexibility but complicating scheduling. Dedicated FMS is tailored for specific products, ensuring efficiency for high-volume items. Engineered FMS involves custom-designed systems for unique processes, while Modular FMS comprises interchangeable modules, promoting scalability and ease of reconfiguration to meet changing production demands.</p></p>
