<p><p>Electroluminescent Displays (ELD) are flat panel displays that utilize the electroluminescence phenomenon to produce light. These displays consist of a phosphor layer in contact with electrodes; when an electric field is applied, the phosphor emits light. ELDs are known for their high brightness, wide viewing angles, and low power consumption, making them suitable for various applications including automotive dashboards, portable devices, and advertising signage.</p><p>The Electroluminescent Display Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.00% during the forecast period, driven by advances in display technology and the increasing demand for energy-efficient and lightweight display solutions. Additionally, the rising popularity of IoT devices is prompting manufacturers to explore innovative applications for ELDs. The automotive sector is likely to be a significant growth driver, as ELDs are increasingly incorporated into vehicle infotainment and navigation systems.
