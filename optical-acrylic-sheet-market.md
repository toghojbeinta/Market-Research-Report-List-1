<p><strong>The Optical Acrylic Sheet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extruded Acrylic Sheet</li><li>Cast Acrylic Sheet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical acrylic sheet market comprises two primary types: extruded acrylic sheets and cast acrylic sheets. Extruded acrylic sheets are produced by forcing acrylic resin through a heated chamber, resulting in consistent thickness and a more cost-effective option, widely used in applications like signage and displays. Cast acrylic sheets, on the other hand, are made by pouring liquid acrylic into molds, offering superior optical clarity and customization, making them ideal for high-end applications such as lighting and decorative features.</p></p>
