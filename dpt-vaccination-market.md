<p><p>The DPT vaccination market comprises three main types: DTaP, Tdap, and DTwP. DTaP is a pediatric vaccine that protects against diphtheria, tetanus, and pertussis for children under seven years. Tdap is an adolescent and adult booster that reduces disease transmission while maintaining immunity. DTwP, which is less common due to potential side effects, contains whole-cell pertussis antigens. Together, these vaccines play a crucial role in public health by preventing these serious infections across various age groups.</p></p>
