<p><p>The suction diffusers market can be categorized into two main types: integral vane and separate vane diffusers. Integral vane diffusers feature integrated vanes within their structure, promoting streamlined flow and minimizing turbulence, enhancing overall efficiency. In contrast, separate vane diffusers have detachable or separately mounted vanes, allowing for greater flexibility in installation and customization. Both types aim to improve liquid flow management in various applications, optimizing performance and reducing energy consumption in HVAC and industrial systems.</p></p>
