<p><p>Wireless broadband Internet is categorized into two main types: fixed and mobile. Fixed wireless broadband delivers high-speed internet to a specific location, utilizing radio signals from a base station, making it ideal for rural and underserved areas. Conversely, mobile wireless broadband provides internet access on-the-go via cellular networks, enabling users to connect from various locations with portable devices. Both markets cater to different consumer needs, offering flexibility and accessibility while enhancing connectivity in diverse environments.</p></p>
