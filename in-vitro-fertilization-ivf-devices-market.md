<p><ul><li>Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection (ICSI)</li><li>Frozen Embryo Replacement (FER)</li><li>Pre-implantation Genetic Diagnosis (PGD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The In-Vitro Fertilization (IVF) devices market includes several advanced techniques. Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection (ICSI) involves directly injecting a single sperm into an egg to enhance fertilization, particularly for male infertility issues. Frozen Embryo Replacement (FER) allows the use of previously frozen embryos for implantation, offering flexibility in timing and increasing success rates. Pre-implantation Genetic Diagnosis (PGD) screens embryos for genetic abnormalities before transfer, ensuring healthier pregnancies and reducing the risk of hereditary diseases, thus supporting reproductive choices.</p></p>
