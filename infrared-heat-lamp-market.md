<p><p>The infrared heat lamp market is categorized into three types: Near Infrared, Medium Infrared, and Far Infrared. Near Infrared lamps emit shorter wavelengths and are primarily used for therapeutic applications, providing targeted heat for pain relief and muscle relaxation. Medium Infrared lamps offer a balanced heat distribution, ideal for industrial and culinary uses. Far Infrared lamps produce longer wavelengths, penetrating deeper into the skin, making them popular for wellness therapies and saunas, promoting relaxation and improved circulation.</p></p>
