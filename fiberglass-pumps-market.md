<p><p>The fiberglass pumps market is primarily divided into horizontal and vertical pumps based on their design and application. Horizontal pumps operate with a horizontal shaft, making them suitable for transporting fluids over long distances and commonly used in industrial and agricultural sectors. In contrast, vertical pumps feature a vertical shaft, ideal for applications requiring high fluid lift and minimal footprint, such as in wells and tanks. Both types leverage fiberglass's corrosion resistance and lightweight properties, driving their adoption in demanding environments.</p></p>
