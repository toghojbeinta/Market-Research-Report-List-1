<p><p>Surface roughness measuring gauges are essential tools for assessing the finish quality of materials. The market is divided into two main types: contact and non-contact. Contact gauges utilize mechanical probes that physically touch the surface, providing precise measurements of roughness and texture. In contrast, non-contact gauges employ laser or optical technology to analyze surface characteristics without physical contact, making them ideal for delicate or soft materials. Both types serve crucial roles in manufacturing and quality control processes across various industries.</p></p>
