<p><p>The medical glass bottle market comprises primarily two types: moulded bottles and tube-type bottles. Moulded bottles are crafted by shaping molten glass in molds, allowing for various sizes and designs tailored to specific pharmaceutical needs. Tube-type bottles, on the other hand, are elongated and often used for packaging liquid medications or chemicals. Both types offer excellent chemical resistance and are ideal for preserving the integrity of sensitive contents, making them essential in healthcare and pharmaceutical applications.</p></p>
