<p><strong>The AC Isolators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Break Isolator</li><li>Double Break Isolator</li><li>Pantograph type Isolator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AC isolators market comprises three primary types: Single Break Isolators, Double Break Isolators, and Pantograph Type Isolators. Single Break Isolators interrupt current flow at one point, ensuring a compact design, while Double Break Isolators provide enhanced reliability by breaking the circuit at two points, suitable for higher voltage applications. Pantograph Type Isolators, commonly used in rail systems, feature a flexible design that allows for easy connection and disconnection under varying conditions. Each type serves distinct operational needs in electrical distribution.</p></p>
