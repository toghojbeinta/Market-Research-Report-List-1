<p><p>The HSLA steel market includes several specialized types: </p><p>1. **Weathering Steels** are designed for outdoor exposure, developing a protective rust layer that enhances durability.</p><p>2. **As-Rolled Pearlitic Steels** provide strength with improved toughness through their microstructure.</p><p>3. **Dual-Phase Steels** combine high strength and ductility, making them ideal for automotive applications.</p><p>4. **Inclusion-Shape-Controlled Steels** enhance mechanical properties by controlling non-metallic inclusions, leading to better performance in demanding environments. Each type targets specific industrial applications, emphasizing strength, durability, and efficiency.</p></p>
