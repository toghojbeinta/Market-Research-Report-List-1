<p><p>Electric tube furnaces are categorized into two main types: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal tube furnaces feature a long, cylindrical design oriented horizontally, allowing for easy loading and unloading of materials. They are ideal for processes requiring uniform heating over extended lengths. In contrast, vertical tube furnaces are designed to operate in a vertical position, optimizing space and enabling effective heating of smaller samples. Each type serves specific industrial applications, catering to diverse heating requirements and production settings.</p></p>
