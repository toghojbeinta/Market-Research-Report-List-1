<p><p>The CCTV surveillance system market is primarily categorized into three types: analog, digital, and network. Analog systems use traditional cameras that transmit video signals through coaxial cables, offering basic monitoring capabilities. Digital systems provide enhanced quality and storage options, often utilizing hard drives for recording. Network systems, also known as IP cameras, operate over the internet, enabling remote access and advanced features such as high-resolution imaging and motion detection. Each type addresses varying security needs and technological preferences.</p></p>
