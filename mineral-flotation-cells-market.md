<p><p>Mineral flotation cells are specialized equipment used in the mineral processing industry to separate valuable minerals from ore. These cells utilize the principle of buoyancy, where air is injected into a mixture of ground ore and water, creating bubbles that attach to the targeted minerals, enabling their extraction.
