<p><p>The vaccine separation and purification equipment market comprises three key types: primary separation, refined separation, and purified separation. Primary separation involves initial removal of cell debris and large particle contaminants, ensuring a clearer product. Refined separation further enhances purity by targeting specific impurities, often using techniques like chromatography. Purified separation ensures the final vaccine product meets stringent quality criteria, focusing on removing residual impurities and achieving high levels of purity, essential for safety and efficacy in vaccinations.</p></p>
