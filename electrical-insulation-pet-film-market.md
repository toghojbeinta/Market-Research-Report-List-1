<p><p>The electrical insulation PET film market comprises several types, each serving distinct applications. PPEK (Polyether ether ketone) film is known for its superior mechanical properties and high-temperature resistance, ideal for demanding electrical applications. PVF (Polyvinyl fluoride) film provides excellent electrical insulation and weather resistance, often used in outdoor applications. Other films encompass various specialized materials tailored to specific insulation needs in electrical and electronic sectors.</p></p>
