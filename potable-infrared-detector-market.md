<p><strong>The Potable Infrared Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Thermal Infrared Detector</li><li>Passive Thermal Infrared Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The potable infrared detector market is categorized into active and passive thermal infrared detectors. Active thermal infrared detectors emit their own infrared radiation to detect objects by measuring the radiation reflected back. They are often used in applications requiring precise distance measurements. In contrast, passive thermal infrared detectors sense the infrared radiation naturally emitted by objects within their field of view, making them ideal for surveillance and monitoring applications. Both types play crucial roles in safety, security, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
