<p><ul><li>Dry Friction Plate</li><li>Wet Friction Plate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Clutch Friction Plate market is categorized into two main types: dry and wet friction plates. Dry friction plates operate in a dry environment, typically used in standard automotive applications, offering robust performance and ease of maintenance. In contrast, wet friction plates are immersed in lubricant, which enhances cooling and reduces wear, making them ideal for high-performance vehicles and heavy-duty applications. Each type serves distinct needs based on operating conditions and performance requirements, influencing vehicle efficiency and durability.</p></p>
