<p><strong>The Cell Phone Cellular Repeaters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Analog Signal Booster</li><li>Digital Signal Booster</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cell phone cellular repeaters enhance mobile signal strength by amplifying incoming signals. There are two main types: analog signal boosters and digital signal boosters. Analog signal boosters amplify existing analog signals, making them suitable for older cellular technologies but less efficient for modern networks. In contrast, digital signal boosters process and amplify digital signals, ensuring better quality and bandwidth for contemporary systems. Both types play crucial roles in improving connectivity in areas with weak cellular reception.</p></p>
