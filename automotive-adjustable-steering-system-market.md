<p><strong>The Automotive Adjustable Steering System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Manually Adjustable Steering</li><li>Electrically Adjustable Steering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive adjustable steering system market comprises two main types: manually adjustable steering and electrically adjustable steering. Manually adjustable steering systems rely on physical mechanisms, allowing drivers to change the steering wheel's position using levers or knobs for personalized comfort and ergonomics. In contrast, electrically adjustable steering systems utilize electric motors to facilitate easy and precise adjustments with minimal effort, often integrating features like memory settings for optimal driver preferences. These systems enhance driving experience and accommodate diverse driver needs.</p></p>
