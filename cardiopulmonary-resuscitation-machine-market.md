<p><p>The cardiopulmonary resuscitation (CPR) machine market is categorized into two primary types: mechanical and automated CPR machines. Mechanical CPR machines provide consistent compressions through a mechanical device that mimics manual chest compressions, ensuring effective blood circulation during cardiac arrest. In contrast, automated CPR machines utilize advanced technologies to monitor and adjust compression depth and rate, enhancing the efficacy of resuscitation efforts. Both types aim to improve patient outcomes and are increasingly adopted in emergency medical services and hospitals.</p></p>
