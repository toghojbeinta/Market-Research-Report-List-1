<p><p>The Portable WiFi market is categorized into two main types: Wireless Portable WiFi and Wired Portable WiFi. Wireless Portable WiFi devices, such as mobile hotspots, provide internet access via cellular networks, enabling users to connect multiple devices on the go without the need for cables. Both types cater to different user needs, enhancing connectivity options.</p></p>
