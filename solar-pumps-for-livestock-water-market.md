<p><p>Solar pumps for livestock water are categorized into two main types: submersible and surface pumps. Submersible solar pumps are designed to be submerged in water sources, drawing water from deep wells or boreholes. They are efficient for remote areas with challenging access to electricity. Surface solar pumps, on the other hand, are installed above ground and typically pump water from rivers, ponds, or tanks. Both types harness solar energy, offering sustainable and cost-effective solutions for livestock watering needs.</p></p>
