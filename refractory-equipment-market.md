<p><p>The refractory equipment market encompasses various technologies designed for processing refractory materials. Impact mills are used for crushing and grinding materials, producing fine powders. Air separators efficiently classify particles by size, enhancing overall material quality. Pendulum roller mills excel in achieving precise particle size reduction using swinging rollers, ideal for dense materials. Ring-roll mills provide a more uniform particle size through a rotating mechanism, suitable for a range of refractory applications, thus improving performance and efficiency across the industry.</p></p>
