<p><p>The Distributed Fiber Optic System market is categorized into two primary types based on application and technology: m (meter) and mm (millimeter). The meter-based systems are typically utilized for monitoring and sensing applications over longer distances, such as in pipelines and underground infrastructures. In contrast, millimeter systems are designed for high-resolution sensing at shorter ranges, making them ideal for applications like temperature monitoring and structural health in construction. Both types offer advantages in data transmission and real-time monitoring capabilities.</p></p>
