<p><ul><li>Filters for IPS Screens</li><li>Filters for TN Screens</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LCD panel color filter market is segmented into three main types based on screen technology: filters for IPS screens, filters for TN screens, and others. Filters for IPS (In-Plane Switching) screens enhance color accuracy and viewing angles, catering to high-end displays used in smartphones and monitors. TN (Twisted Nematic) filters are cost-effective but offer limited color reproduction and viewing angles, suitable for budget devices. The "Others" category includes specialized filters for unique technologies like VA (Vertical Alignment) and OLED screens.</p></p>
